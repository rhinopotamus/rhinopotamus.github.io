Remark 2.4.1.

Here's the logic of what we just did: first we showed that if we had a minimizing arc \(E_{12}\text{,}\) then it was necessary for \(E_{12}\) to be a straight line -- that is, "if minimizing arc, then straight line."

Then we showed that if \(C_{12}\) was any other admissible arc (which we can think of as some variation of \(E_{12}\)), then it was definitely longer than \(E_{12}\text{,}\) unless the wiggle was 0. That is, we showed "if straight line, then minimizing arc."
