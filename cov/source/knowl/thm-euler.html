Theorem 3.3.1.

For every minimizing arc \(E_{12}\) there must exist a constant \(c\) such that the equation

\begin{equation} f_{y'}=\int_{x_1}^x f_y\, dx + c\tag{3.3.4} \end{equation}

holds at every point of \(E_{12}.\) On each sub-arc of \(E_{12}\) where the tangent turns continuously we must have

\begin{equation} \frac{d}{dx} f_{y'} = f_y.\tag{3.3.5} \end{equation}
